Our Story

Art has no
boundaries.

Kalasheel was born from a simple, powerful idea: movement transforms lives.

Why we started.

In 2019, a small group of dancers and teachers came together in Lucknow with a shared frustration: quality dance education was either inaccessible, unaffordable, or rooted in gatekeeping. Classical forms felt locked away in conservatories. Modern styles lacked depth.

Kalasheel — a Sanskrit-derived name meaning "one who is skilled in the arts" — was our answer. A studio that holds classical knowledge with reverence but opens its doors without condition.

Five years on, we have trained over 500 students, staged 30+ performances, and built a community that spans classical arangetrams and street dance battles alike.

"We built Kalasheel because we believe every person has a dancer inside them. Our only job is to help them find it."

— Founder, Kalasheel
What We Stand For

Our principles.

Age, background, body type — none of it determines who can dance. Every student is met where they are.

Kalasheel is a place, a practice and a people. We celebrate together, grow together, perform together.

Our Journey

Five years of movement.

2019

Founded

Kalasheel opens with 3 instructors and 40 students in a single studio space in Lucknow.

2020

Online During Lockdown

Pandemic pivots Kalasheel to digital classes. Reaches students across 8 cities. Community grows stronger.

2021

First Annual Performance

Tarangini — Kalasheel's first full-scale annual showcase. 120 performers, 600 audience members.

2023

Expanded to 12 Styles

New instructors, new forms. Odissi, Folk, Breaking and Theatre Movement added to the programme.

2024

500+ Students Trained

Kalasheel reaches a milestone. A new children's wing opens. The community keeps growing.
